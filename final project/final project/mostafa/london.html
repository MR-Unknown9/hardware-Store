London Eye

A huge tourist wheel or ferris wheel was created in the British capital before the beginning of the twenty-first century. It is one of London's tourist attractions and is located on the banks of the River Thames. A person can ride it to see the sights of the city of London and enjoy the beautiful scenery as it rotates around its axis. Wikipedia

Tower of London

Officially, Her Majesty's Palace and Castle is a historic castle on the north bank of the River Thames in the heart of London, England. The Tower of London was built in late 1066, as part of the Norman conquest of England. In 1078, William the Conqueror ordered the construction of the White Tower, which became a symbol of the new dynasty's conquest of their enemies in London.
